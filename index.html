Resumé

I am a data and insight professional based out of Portland, OR.

I have worked in healthcare for over 10 years: 5 years in direct patient care and 5 years in administrative roles with a focus on data management and reporting.

I specialize in gathering quantitative data from disparate sources and using it to drive consumer-focused and operational research projects. I have experience in nearly every step in the insight gathering process, including data mining, modelling, reporting, analysis, and presentation. I also have experience leading multifunctional team projects aimed at improving consumer experience.

Beyond my professional experience, I have created multiple interactive experiences and shared them with friends and family. These include role-playing game systems, escape rooms, and board games.

I have also dabbled in web design, graphic design, and audio recording in both professional and personal settings.
